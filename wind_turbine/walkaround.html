What it is
A commercial wind turbine converts wind energy into electrical energy.
What it does
The rotor captures wind energy, the drivetrain transfers it, and the electrical system sends power to the grid.
Why a technician cares
Technicians inspect, maintain, and troubleshoot each of these systems to keep the turbine safe and producing power.
